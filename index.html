Kansas City Business Journal Mixed-UseCapstone Award

One Nine Vine recently received the 2024 Kansas City Business Journal Mixed-Use Capstone Award for its impactful contribution to Kansas City’s 18th and VineDistrict, the historic birthplace of jazz and acenter for African-American businesses.

Completed in October 2023, One Nine Vineis a six-story, 81,550 sq. ft. mixed-usedevelopment featuring 17,500 sq. ft. of retailspace and 80 apartments, with every unit including a balcony, some offering views ofthe Kansas City skyline as the first phase of alarger 400-unit master plan. As the first multi-family market-rate housingproject in the Jazz District since 2006, theproject exceeded its 17% MBE participation goal, achieving 30% MBE and 6% WBE.

This demonstrates a strong commitment todiversity and community development.
