What We Look For: Investment Criteria

1. Asset Types

  • 🌱
    Undeveloped / underutilized land
  • 🏘️
    Multifamily & BFR sites
  • ⛪
    Church / institutional land
  • 📍
    Infill parcels with zoning upside
  • 🏗️
    Income properties with potential

2. Scale & Density

  • 📐
    Units: 120–550 (phased)
  • 🏘️
    Density: 8–25 units/acre
  • 🌐
    Land: 10–60 acres

3. Geography

  • 🌴
    South Florida: Fort Lauderdale, Broward
  • ☀️
    Central Florida: Tampa, Orlando, Ocala
  • 🏙️
    Midwest: Kansas City, St. Louis, Columbus

4. Performance Targets

  • 📈
    IRR: 20%+
  • 💰
    Equity Multiple: 1.8x+
  • ⏳
    Hold Period: 1–10 years
  • 🚪
    Exit: Entitlement sale, JV, build, or hold

5. Land & Location

  • 💧
    Utilities: Water, sewer, power access
  • 🧾
    Zoning: By-right or feasible path
  • 🏛️
    Public Incentives: TIF, CRA, HUD, grants
  • 🏢
    Employment Drivers: Near major employers, schools, hospitals
  • 🔄
    Exit Flexibility: Phase, JV, hold or sell
