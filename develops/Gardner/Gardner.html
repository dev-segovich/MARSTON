Waverly Plaza is a two-phase multifamily development located in the thriving community of Gardner, Kansas City, designed to offer modern living spaces alongside vibrant community amenities. Phase I features 168 residential units, while Phase II adds 159 units across three thoughtfully designed buildings. Residents enjoy access to a state-of-the-art clubhouse equipped with premium amenities, creating a welcoming and elevated lifestyle. Looking ahead, Waverly Plaza will also incorporate retail space, adding even greater convenience and accessibility for its residents. This project reflects our mission of creating cozy, functional spaces that enhance suburban living.

Strategically positioned near Interstate 35, Waverly Plaza seamlessly connects residents to major employment hubs, essential services, and recreational opportunities while preserving the charm of suburban living. The future addition of retail spaces further enhances its value, offering a unique blend of housing and lifestyle convenience. With its modern design, community-driven features, and a prime location, Waverly Plaza is an exceptional choice for residents seeking a seamless lifestyle and for investors aiming for long-term growth in a high-demand market.
